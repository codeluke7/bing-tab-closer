Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Bing Tab Closer does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

Data Collection

This extension does not collect any user data.

Permissions

The extension uses the tabs permission solely to identify and close open Bing tabs when the user clicks the extension button.

No Tracking

This extension does not use analytics, tracking scripts, remote code, or external network requests.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you may contact the developer at the email address listed on the Chrome Web Store page.
